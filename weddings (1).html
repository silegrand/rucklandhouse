The Setting Is Ready.
Are You?

"A building that has held generations of celebrations — and is ready to hold yours."

Ruckland Rooms has been at the heart of Gravesend since 1905. Its rooms have seen decades of toasts, first dances, and tearful speeches. That history isn't just atmosphere — it's the quiet confidence of a venue that knows how to hold a great occasion.

We offer two clear routes. Take our fully inclusive package and let us handle everything — catering, bar, décor essentials, entertainment, and a dedicated coordinator who's been doing this for years. Or hire the room on your own terms, bring your own suppliers, and build the day piece by piece. Either way, the kitchen, the bar, and the team are yours.

There's no corporate events manager here passing your file between departments. When you call us, you speak to Tracy. She's planned hundreds of weddings at Ruckland Rooms and she'll remember yours long after it's done.

Common Questions

Good to Know Before You Book

How many guests can Ruckland Rooms accommodate? +

The Ruckland Room seats up to 120 guests for a formal sit-down wedding breakfast, or accommodates up to 180 for a buffet-style or evening reception. For smaller, more intimate gatherings, the Pocahontas Room with its adjoining Lounge Bar is an excellent alternative.

Do we have to use your caterer? +

On a room-hire basis, you're welcome to bring an approved external caterer — Tracy can provide a list. That said, most couples find our in-house team provides everything they need at excellent value, and the convenience of everything under one roof is genuinely hard to beat.

Is there a minimum number of guests? +

We don't impose a strict minimum, but our pricing works best from around 50 guests for a wedding breakfast. If you're planning something more intimate, call Tracy and she'll work out what makes sense for your numbers.

Is there parking at the venue? +

Yes — Ruckland Rooms has its own private car park at no extra charge. The venue is also centrally located in Gravesend, close to the railway station, so guests travelling without a car are well catered for too.

What's included in the drinks package? +

The standard drinks package includes an arrival drink, one third of a bottle of wine per person during the wedding breakfast, and a glass of prosecco for the toasting drink. Beyond that, our fully licensed bar operates at pub prices throughout the evening — no hotel-style markups.

How far in advance should we book? +

Popular dates — especially Saturdays between April and October — can book up 12 to 18 months in advance. We'd recommend making an enquiry as soon as you have a date in mind. There's no obligation at the enquiry stage, and checking availability costs nothing.

Can we see the venue before we commit? +

Absolutely — we actively encourage it. Call Tracy to arrange a viewing and she'll show you around both rooms, answer your questions, and walk you through how your day might look. There's never any pressure to book on the spot.
