the Details.

"A familiar, welcoming space where people can gather without fuss — and remember without distraction."

Ruckland Rooms has been at the heart of the Gravesend community for over a century. We understand that when families gather to honour someone they've lost, the surroundings should feel warm and uncomplicated — not formal, not clinical.

Our team has supported many families through this kind of occasion. We approach every wake with the same care we bring to any event — but with a quieter hand. You tell us what you need. We'll make it happen. If you're not sure what you need, we'll help you work it out.

Rooms are available on a half-day or full-day basis. All menus can be adapted to suit the occasion, and every item below is a starting point — not a fixed list.

Practical Information

Common Questions

How quickly can you accommodate a booking?+

We understand that funeral arrangements often move quickly. Please call Tracy directly on 01474 352171 or 07850 48 49 75 — we'll do everything we can to accommodate short-notice bookings and confirm availability as soon as possible.

How many guests can the room hold?+

The Ruckland Room accommodates up to 120 guests seated or 180 for a standing or buffet-style gathering. For smaller, more intimate groups, the Pocahontas Room with its adjoining Lounge Bar is a comfortable alternative.

Can the menu be changed or adapted?+

Yes — every menu is a starting point. Items can be added, removed or swapped to suit dietary requirements or personal wishes. Just ask Tracy and she'll provide a revised menu and quote.

Is the bar available at a wake?+

Yes. Our fully licensed bar is available throughout your gathering if required, staffed by our own team. We can discuss what's appropriate for your occasion when you call.

Is there parking?+

Yes — our private car park is available to all guests free of charge. The venue is also very close to Gravesend railway station for guests travelling by train.
