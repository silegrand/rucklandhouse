Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How many guests can the venue hold for a wedding reception? +

The Ruckland Room accommodates up to 120 guests for formal seated dining, or up to 180 for a buffet-style or standing event. For smaller, more intimate gatherings, the Pocahontas Room with access to the Lounge Bar is an excellent alternative.

Can we use our own caterer? +

On a room-hire basis, yes — we can provide a list of approved external caterers. However, many couples find that our in-house team, with years of wedding experience, provides everything they need at excellent value.

Is there parking on site? +

Yes — Ruckland Rooms has its own private car park, which is available to your guests for the duration of your event. The venue is also close to Gravesend railway station for guests travelling by train.

Do you offer a fully licensed bar? +

Absolutely. Our fully licensed bar is staffed by our own team throughout your event. We serve a range of wines, spirits, ales, lagers, and soft drinks. Bar packages and pre-agreed drink options can be discussed at enquiry.

How far in advance should we book? +

Popular dates — especially Saturdays in summer — can book up well in advance. We'd recommend getting in touch as soon as you have a date in mind. There's no obligation at the enquiry stage, so reach out early and we'll check availability for you.
