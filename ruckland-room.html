The Flagship Space

A Room That
Commands the Occasion.

"High ceilings, a sprung floor, a stage — and a kitchen that genuinely knows how to feed a crowd."

The Ruckland Room is our largest and most versatile space. It has soaring ceilings, natural light on three sides, full air conditioning, and a floor plan generous enough to seat 120 for a sit-down dinner — or welcome 180 for a standing reception. A wide range of table configurations can be achieved to suit your event exactly.

What makes this room different from any hotel ballroom is the building it sits in. Ruckland House has been part of Gravesend life since 1905. The exposed original features, the Victorian bones, the sense that something has happened here before — it all contributes to an atmosphere that no purpose-built conference suite can manufacture.

The room connects directly to the Lounge Bar, and can be combined with the adjacent Pocahontas Room for events that need both a main space and a quieter breakout or catering area. Our in-house team handles everything from the layout to the last plate.
