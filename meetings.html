Common Questions

Good to Know Before You Book

What's the maximum number of delegates?+

The Ruckland Room holds up to 140 in theatre-style seating, or around 120 in a classroom layout. For smaller groups, the Pocahontas Room comfortably fits 20–30. Both rooms can be hired together for events requiring breakout space.

Can we set the room up in a specific layout?+

Yes — our team will set the room in your preferred configuration before your delegates arrive. Common layouts include theatre, classroom, U-shape, boardroom, and cabaret. Just let Tracy know when you book.

Is AV equipment available?+

Please discuss your AV requirements with Tracy when enquiring. We'll confirm what's available and what you may need to bring or arrange. Many clients bring their own laptops and projectors.

Is catering included in the hire price?+

Rooms are available with or without catering — it's entirely flexible. You can add anything from a simple tea and coffee arrangement to a full working lunch. Tracy will price this separately when she provides your quote.

Is there parking for delegates?+

Yes — our private car park is free for all delegates throughout your hire. The venue is also a short walk from Gravesend railway station, making it accessible for those travelling from London or elsewhere in Kent.

Can we hire both rooms at once?+

Absolutely. The Ruckland Room and Pocahontas Room can be hired together — useful for events that need a main conference space and a separate breakout or catering area. Contact Tracy for a combined quote.
