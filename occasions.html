What We Host

Every Reason to Celebrate

Life's milestones deserve more than a back room in a pub. Here's what we do best.

Birthday Parties

Milestone birthdays deserve a milestone venue. The Ruckland Room gives a 40th, 50th or 60th the scale it deserves — with catering, a bar, and a dance floor already taken care of.

Anniversaries

Silver, gold, pearl — every anniversary is a story worth celebrating properly. We'll set the table, stock the bar, and let the two of you enjoy the evening you deserve.

Christenings

A relaxed, welcoming space for family and friends to come together. Our catering team can do anything from a simple fork buffet to a full sit-down lunch for the whole family.

Retirement Parties

Years of hard work deserves a proper send-off. A private room, a well-stocked bar, and a kitchen that can feed a crowd — everything a good retirement party needs.

Community Events

Club dinners, society events, awards evenings — Ruckland Rooms has hosted the Gravesend Masonic community for over a century. Community is in our bones.

Good to Know

Common Questions

How many guests can you accommodate?+

The Ruckland Room holds up to 120 for formal sit-down dining and up to 180 for a buffet or standing event. For smaller gatherings of 20–50 people, the Pocahontas Room with its adjoining Lounge Bar is an ideal and more intimate option.

Can the menus be changed or customised?+

Every menu on this page is a starting point. Our kitchen can substitute items, accommodate dietary requirements (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, allergens), or build something entirely from scratch. Just ask Tracy for a revised quote.

Is there a bar at the venue?+

Yes — Ruckland Rooms has a fully licensed bar staffed by our own team. We serve wines, spirits, ales, lagers, and soft drinks at pub prices throughout your event. No inflated hotel bar prices here.

Is there parking?+

Yes — we have our own private car park available at no charge to your guests. The venue is also centrally located in Gravesend, a short walk from the railway station, so guests travelling by train are well catered for too.

How do I get a quote?+

The quickest route is to call Tracy directly on 01474 352171 or 07850 48 49 75. Alternatively, use the contact form and she'll come back to you promptly. Give her a rough guest count, a date if you have one, and an idea of what you're planning — she'll do the rest.

Can I bring my own cake or decorations?+

Absolutely. This is your event — bring whatever makes it personal. We provide tables, chairs, linen, and a cake stand. Beyond that, the room is yours to dress as you like.
